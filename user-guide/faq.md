# FAQ / Troubleshooting

**Q: I don't see the MDM section in my admin sidebar at all.**
The app may not be installed/linked on your account yet, or your admin user may not have the right role/permissions. Contact the marketplace administrator.

**Q: A product I sell isn't showing up in the Documents search.**
The product must first be linked to your seller account on the MDM side. This linking is done by the marketplace administrator, not from this page.

**Q: My upload failed.**
Check that the file is a PDF under 20 MB and that a document type is selected — both are required before the Upload button is enabled. If it still fails, the error message returned will include a short reason; share that with support if it's unclear.

**Q: I can't delete a document.**
You can only delete documents you uploaded yourself. Documents added by the marketplace or another seller aren't shown to you and can't be deleted from here.

**Q: I can't subscribe to a plan from the Subscription page.**
Correct for now — the Subscription page currently only shows your existing plan (if any) or the available plans; there's no purchase flow wired up on it yet. Contact the marketplace administrator about signing up.

**Q: Can I get a refund or cancel my subscription?**
Not from this page yet — contact the marketplace administrator.

**Q: Is my payment information stored on this site?**
No. This app doesn't collect card details on the Subscription page at all right now.
