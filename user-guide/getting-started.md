# Getting Started

This guide is for **sellers** using the marketplace admin. It covers where to find these tools and what each one does.

## Where to find it

1. Log in to your VTEX Admin at `https://{your-account}.myvtex.com/admin`.
2. In the left sidebar, open the **MDM** section.
3. You'll see:
   - **Documents** — upload and manage SDS / TDS / MSDS PDFs for your products.
   - **Subscription** — view your current marketplace plan, status, and invoices, subscribe if you don't have one yet, or see available plans.
   - **Embed Demo** — a technical demo of a different payment method, unrelated to your actual subscription. Not something you need to use — safe to ignore.

## What you can see

Everything in these pages is scoped to your own seller account:

- On **Documents**, you only see products linked to your seller account, and only documents *you* uploaded — not documents from the marketplace or other sellers.
- On **Subscription**, you only see your own subscription status and billing history.
